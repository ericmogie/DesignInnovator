Ideas

I'm a creative director who believes great work lives at the intersection of craft, strategy, and relentless curiosity.

With 27+ years in design and 20+ years coding, I bring a rare combination of right-brain creativity and left-brain technical thinking to every engagement. I've spent the last 13+ years at Grainger leading creative for one of the world's largest B2B distributors.

I'm deeply invested in the intersection of design and AI — not as a replacement for human creativity, but as an amplifier. I've built custom tools, frameworks, and workflows that help teams do their best work faster and smarter.

When I'm not designing or coding, I'm exploring new AI models, shooting photography, or solving whatever problem happens to be most interesting that day.
